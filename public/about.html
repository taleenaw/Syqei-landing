Meet the founder

Hi, I'm Taleena.

20 year old Thai-Australian Computer Science & Cybersecurity student

How It Started

Growing up in regional Western Australia, I was surrounded by incredible nature, but I rarely knew what was happening around me. Wildflowers would bloom, whales would migrate and butterflies would emerge, yet I'd usually find out after the moment had passed. Like a lot of people my age, I also spent far more time on my phone than I did exploring the world just outside my door.

That sparked a simple question: what if technology could help us reconnect with nature instead of distracting us from it?

Making It Happen

Syqei helps people discover what's happening in nature nearby, right when it matters most. From blooming wildflowers to migrating whales, every real-world visit becomes part of your own living garden through scientifically accurate butterflies connected to that place and season.

Why "Syqei"

Syqei is pronounced psy-key, inspired by Psyche, whose name means both "soul" and "butterfly" in Ancient Greek. Butterflies have long symbolised transformation, making them the perfect way to remember the places and moments that change us.

Technology has become very good at capturing our attention.

I hope Syqei helps direct some of it back to the natural world.

— Taleena Watts

Founder, Syqei
