For parks, gardens & nature reserves

Bring Syqei's community to your gates.

Syqei turns real visits to real places into a lasting personal memory — a scientifically accurate butterfly, tied to the day someone actually showed up. We're looking for a handful of WA venues to pilot this with first.

Start the conversation

Drive real visitation

Syqei tells people what's worth seeing at your site right now — wildflowers peaking, a species active this week — and gives them a reason to come this weekend, not "someday."

Understand your visitors

Verified visit data gives you a clearer picture of who's coming, when, and what draws them back — insight most venues don't currently have easy access to.

Encourage good visitor behaviour

Unlock bonus rewards for staying on trail, completing a short conservation prompt, or reporting an invasive species — turning visitor engagement into visitor management.

Ways to Partner

Verified Visit Program

A simple QR or geofence check-in at your site unlocks a species matched to your location and the current season — no app development required on your end.

Sponsored Species Set

Feature species unique to your venue, with your site name attached to every unlock — a co-branded moment for every visitor who discovers it there.

Seasonal Journey

A limited-time event tied to something real happening at your site — wildflower season, a migration window — to drive a spike in visits during your peak period.

Conservation Storytelling

Bite-sized Field Guide facts about your site's species and conservation status, woven into the moment someone unlocks them — education that doesn't feel like homework.
