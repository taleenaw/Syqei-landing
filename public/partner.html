About Partner with us

For organisations connecting people with nature

Become part of Australia's living map of nature.

We're partnering with parks, gardens, wildlife attractions, tourism organisations, councils and conservation groups to build a new way for people to discover what's happening in nature, right when it matters most.

Become a launch partner

Why partner with Syqei

Drive real visitation

Give people a reason to visit today, not someday. Seasonal moments create genuine urgency. Whether it's wildflowers peaking, whales migrating or butterflies emerging, Syqei helps people discover these experiences while they're still happening.

Understand how people explore

Learn which seasonal moments inspire visits, which experiences resonate most, and how visitors engage throughout the year using privacy-first, anonymous insights.

Turn visits into conservation

Encourage visitors to stay on marked trails, discover native species, participate in citizen science and connect more deeply with the places they visit.

Our vision

We believe every park, reserve, garden and coastline has incredible stories unfolding every week. Our goal is to make those stories visible, helping more people discover, appreciate and protect the places around them.

Or your vision.

Have a different idea for how this could work at your site? We're a small, WA-based team building this from the ground up, and we'd genuinely love to hear it.
